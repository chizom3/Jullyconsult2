Why Do We Collect Personal Data

JullyConsult Venture collects and processes personal data strictly for legitimate business and operational purposes, including:

  • Fulfillment of contractual obligations;

We process personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently.

Data Retention Period

Personal data will be processed and stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected.

Where required for legal, accounting, contractual, or regulatory purposes, personal data may be retained for up to five (5) years following the termination of business or contractual relationships, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

After the applicable retention period, personal data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

Transferring Personal Data to Third Parties

JullyConsult Venture processes personal data under controlled access conditions.

We do not transfer personal data to third parties except:

  • When legally required by regulatory authorities, tax authorities, or courts;
  • When necessary for contractual performance (e.g., logistics providers, payment processors, grant partners);
  • When consent has been provided by the data subject.

In all cases, appropriate safeguards are applied to protect personal data.

Your Rights Regarding Your Personal Data

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about how your personal data is collected and used;
  • Access your personal data;
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data;
  • Request restriction of processing;
  • Withdraw your consent (where processing is based on consent);
  • Object to processing under certain circumstances;
  • Request portability of your personal data to another service provider;
  • Know whether your personal data is subject to automated processing or profiling;
  • File a complaint with a relevant data protection supervisory authority.

These rights may be exercised in accordance with applicable laws and while ensuring JullyConsult Venture's legitimate interests.
