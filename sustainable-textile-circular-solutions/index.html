About us

JullyConsult is a leading waste circularity, education, and fashion-tech social enterprise creating zero-waste clothing with AI-powered design tools. We transform discarded textiles and fast fashion waste into stylish garments, reusable pads, bags, berets, lifestyle products, and artworks; proving sustainability and creativity can go hand in hand. Our patent AI technology automates zero-waste pattern design, enabling entire fabrics to be used with minimal cutting, inspired by traditional garment construction. Beyond design, we deliver end-to-end circular solutions including textile collection, sorting, repair, recycling, and upcycling in partnership with fashion brands and textile industries.

And through our Fashion Lab and Upcycling Hub, we promote non-formal education, vocational training, and lifelong learning. We educate, train, and empower women, youth, Internally Displaced Persons (IDPs), Persons with Disabilities (PWDs), and out-of-school children with hands-on skills in sustainable fashion, creativity, textile/plastic recycling, and entrepreneurship.

We integrate solar-powered equipment into our production and training processes, reducing greenhouse gas emissions while ensuring accessible, inclusive training and manufacturing. JullyConsult combines climate action, innovation, non-formal education, and social impact to advance textile circularity and build a more sustainable and inclusive fashion ecosystem.

Vission

To shape and lead a global transformation in the fashion ecosystem where zero-waste design, circular innovation, and inclusive non-formal education drive sustainable livelihoods, climate resilience, and equal opportunities for all; and green production become the standard, creating a world where textiles are continuously repurposed, communities are empowered, and climate impact is significantly reduced.

Mission

To eliminate textile waste through AI-powered zero-waste design, upcycling discarded fabrics into high-quality fashion products, and delivering end-to-end circular solutions, while advancing non-formal vocational education that equips women, youth, IDPs, PWDs, and out-of-school children with practical skills, clean energy tools, and access to sustainable economic opportunities and financing.

PARTNERS

RECOGNITION
World Sustainability Award

JullyConsult has been recognized for its commitment to sustainable innovation and circular fashion solutions. Our work focuses on reducing textile waste through zero-waste design powered by AI technology, alongside upcycling initiatives that repair and remake discarded clothing. Through community workshops, we share these skills with both children and adults while supporting charitable causes and environmental education.

We actively participate in global and local events, campaigns, and initiatives that promote sustainable consumption, and circular economy solutions. To spread awareness about sustainable production, usage and textile recovery solutions.


Global Youth Environmental Change Award, United Kingdom

Our leadership in climate action and sustainability has received international recognition, including the Global Youth Environmental Change Award in the United Kingdom. This recognition highlights our efforts to address environmental challenges through innovative textile recycling, community empowerment, and sustainable production practices.


Anzisha Prize Fellowship with African Leadership Academy, South Africa

JullyConsult’s founder was selected for the Anzisha Prize Fellowship, a prestigious program that recognizes and supports Africa’s most promising young entrepreneurs creating impactful ventures.

This recognition highlights our work in advancing education, health, and employability through green vocational training programs for unemployed women and youth in low-income communities and Internally Displaced Persons (IDP) camps. Through these programs, we train participants to produce antibacterial reusable sanitary pads, bags, and other products using fabric, plastic, and textile waste.

Our initiative addresses period poverty, ensuring that girls and women have access to affordable and hygienic menstrual products while also creating sustainable livelihood opportunities.


Access Bank Womenpreneur Recognition

JullyConsult has also been recognized under the Access Bank Womenpreneur initiative, which celebrates women-led businesses that drive innovation, economic empowerment, and social impact.

This recognition acknowledges our work in building a circular fashion enterprise that empowers women through skills development, entrepreneurship support, and sustainable production. Through our programs, women gain practical skills in textile upcycling, fashion design, and green manufacturing, enabling them to create income-generating opportunities while contributing to environmental sustainability.
