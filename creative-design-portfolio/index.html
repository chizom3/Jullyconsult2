JullyConsult Creative Design Portfolio: Sustainable Fashion and Circular Products

Explore JullyConsult's real design galleries, organised with the properly named images from the design folder and grouped into hand fans, outfits, Ankara bags, Cultural Identity Ankara bags, earrings, accessories, berets, footwears, artworks, and shredded special design bags.

This portfolio shows how recovered textile materials become useful, beautiful, and culturally expressive products through craftsmanship, reuse, and circular design thinking.
