COLLECTION

We deploy multiple, accessible collection channels to divert as much textile waste as possible from landfills. Through partnerships with households, tailoring shops, fashion brands, retailers, and communities, we recover discarded clothing, production offcuts, post-consumer textiles, and plastics for responsible processing and reuse.

Street Collection

Our street collection system uses strategically placed waste carts in high-traffic areas where passersby, households, and individuals deposit textile and plastic waste for scheduled pickup. Beyond simple collection, we manage the full textile recovery process, including maintenance, public communication and engagement, data tracking, and reporting. This service also provides tailored solutions for localities and organizations seeking sustainable waste management.

In-Store Collection

Our in-store collection targets tailoring shops and fashion brands, gathering leftover fabric and production waste. Designed as a one-stop solution, this service helps retailers implement textile sustainability initiatives while reducing waste from their operations.

Second-Hand Collection

We collect second-hand clothing from markets, including Kantamanto Market in Accra, Ghana, and various markets across Nigeria. These items are transported through licensed channels and repurposed through textile recycling and shredding to create new garments and fashion products.

Industrial Textile Collection

We partner with textile manufacturers to recover post-industrial fabric waste and leftover materials. This service addresses the collection and circular recovery needs of industries, transforming their production remnants into new, sustainable designs.
