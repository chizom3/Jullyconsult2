Textile Eco-Green Calculator

What resources do you help save by disposing of textiles and plastics separately?

The Textile Eco-Green Calculator measures and shows the environmental impact and savings achieved when textiles and plastics are properly separated from general waste.

By using this tool, you contribute to a more sustainable future through reuse of textile products, reducing the need for producing new materials and conserving natural resources such as water, energy, and raw fibers.

It also highlights the value of recycling and recovery of textile waste, preventing unnecessary disposal in landfills and reducing environmental pollution.

  • Water savings
  • Energy savings
  • Lower emissions
  • Raw fiber conservation
  • Less landfill waste
  • Reduced pollution

Why separate disposal matters

Proper separation creates value before waste reaches landfill. It supports reuse, recovery, cleaner communities, and better evidence of environmental benefit.

Reuse first

Textile items that can be reused help avoid the environmental cost of manufacturing new products from virgin materials.

Recovery and recycling

Separating textiles and plastics from mixed waste improves the quality of materials that can be recycled or recovered.

Measurable ESG value

The calculator turns everyday disposal actions into visible environmental metrics that can support awareness, reporting, and partnerships.
