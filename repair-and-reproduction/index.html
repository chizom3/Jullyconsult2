REPAIR AND REPRODUCTION

Cleaning and Redesign

To keep clothing in circulation and extend the life of textiles, JullyConsult has developed in-house cleaning and redesign services. After the textile sorting services process, reusable fabrics/ garments are carefully cleaned and transformed into new fashion items through creative redesign. For example, denim/jean trousers are repurposed (cut, crafted and designed) into stylish bags or other contemporary accessories; textile leftovers used to create reusable-pads with anti-bacteria materials. For textiles that are too damaged for direct reuse, we apply a different circular approach. These fabrics are shredded and reconstructed into new products such as jackets, bags, and other functional lifestyle items. Through this process, we prevent valuable materials from ending up in landfills while giving discarded textiles a new life through innovative design and sustainable production.
