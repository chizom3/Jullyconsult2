Participation in Eco Events

We collaborate with non-governmental organizations, municipalities, and educational institutions in events that raise awareness about responsible consumption and sustainable fashion.
Our partnerships unite the efforts of various sectors to reduce textile waste.

Educational Campaigns

Our campaigns, held in schools, universities, and at the Jully Consult base, explain the principles of the circular economy as well as the possibilities for recycling and reusing textiles. We share knowledge on extending the lifecycle of clothing and footwear and use interactive tools to encourage lasting changes in consumer behavior.

Collaboration with Business

Through partnerships with fashion brands, retail chains, and manufacturers, we promote sustainable practices in the textile industry. We offer comprehensive textile waste management, support for communication strategies and branding, as well as logistical assistance for collecting unwanted textiles, ensuring efficiency, transparency, and high-quality services.
