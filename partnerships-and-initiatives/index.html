Initiatives And Eco Events

From children’s workshops to participation in international projects, we believe that through joint efforts, we can achieve real change. Would you like to be part of it?    Contact us, and together, let’s create a positive impact on the environment!

EcoGreen Academy

Catch Them Young;  Raising Climate-Conscious Leaders

EcoGreen Academy is our educational arm focused on school children and out-of-school youth.

We provide environmental education (EE) on:

  • Textile waste and its impact
  • Sustainable living practices
  • Recycling, reuse, and upcycling

Through interactive workshops, we teach children about SDGs and  how to:

  • Repair old clothes
  • Transform waste into useful items
  • Practice sustainability from home

Our goal is to nurture a new generation of climate-conscious ambassadors who understand their role in protecting the environment.

Participation in Eco Events

JullyConsult actively collaborates with non-governmental organizations, municipalities, and educational institutions to organize and participate in events that promote responsible consumption and sustainable fashion. Through these partnerships, we bring together diverse stakeholders to raise awareness, inspire action, and drive collective efforts toward reducing textile waste. Our engagement in eco-events strengthens cross-sector collaboration and amplifies the impact of circular economy initiatives.

Educational Campaigns

We implement educational campaigns across schools, universities, and local communities to promote awareness of the circular economy and sustainable textile practices. Our programs focus on:

  • Understanding the environmental impact of textile waste
  • Promoting recycling and reuse of clothing and footwear
  • Encouraging responsible consumption habits
Using interactive tools, workshops, and practical demonstrations, we empower individuals to extend the lifecycle of their clothing and adopt more sustainable behaviors. Our goal is to create long-term mindset shifts that support a culture of sustainability.
