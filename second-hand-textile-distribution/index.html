SEGMENT DISTRIBUTION


Our distribution strategy focuses on identifying the communities and locations where specific products are most needed and where market demand is strongest. Different markets prefer different types of products; for example, some customers prefer shredded-texture materials produced from mixed damaged fabrics, while others prefer redesigned products such as jean/denim bags or recreated garments.

Because fashion preferences vary across regions, cultures, religions, and lifestyles, effective distribution requires deep understanding of local contexts. JullyConsult's national and international experience enables us to identify the most appropriate secondary markets for different types of recovered textiles, ensuring they are reused and valued rather than discarded.

This approach not only strengthens circular fashion systems but also promotes cultural creativity, local identity, and sustainable consumption. With this, we promote Tourism and Culture.
